About

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer.

Good design is key to creating a successful product or service. My job is to understand the users, both internal and external and create a solution that fits within the contraints of the project.

I look into people's motives, behaviours and the environments they sit within to understand how I can best solve their problems and allow them to easily do what they need to.

The projects I enjoy most are ones that improve society or people's lives in some way, however big or small.

A bit about me, I grew up in Hong Kong with Chinese and Irish parents, speaking Cantonese and English at home. I went to the University of Leeds to do a Masters in Product Design and then moved to London and have been living here since.

I enjoy staying active and spending my free time training for triathlons, learning aerial silks and when the opportunity arises, attempt to surf.
